About the Event

Ideas Worth
Spreading

TEDxThiagarajar College of Engineering is a student-driven platform that brings together innovators, entrepreneurs, educators, researchers, artists, and changemakers to inspire the next generation through powerful ideas.

TEDx is a program of independently organized events licensed by TED that brings communities together to share Ideas Worth Spreading through inspiring talks and meaningful conversations.

Hosted at Thiagarajar College of Engineering, the event celebrates innovation, curiosity, leadership, and human potential.

Our Mission

To inspire individuals with ideas that challenge perspectives, encourage innovation, and empower every attendee to become an active contributor to the systems they live in.

Objectives
  • Inspire bold thinking
  • Promote innovation
  • Interdisciplinary collaboration
  • Empower future leaders
  • Build changemakers
  • Celebrate ideas
Theme 2026

We Are The System

Systems are often seen as something external — governments, institutions, organizations, or technology. But every system is ultimately built, sustained, and transformed by people.

We Are The System is a call to recognize our role in shaping the world around us. Every decision, every innovation, every conversation, and every act of leadership contributes to the systems we live in.

This theme invites us to rethink responsibility, embrace collaboration, and realize that meaningful change begins not with someone else, but with each one of us.

"Because the most powerful system is the one we choose to build together."
